Services and Pricing

BoatBuddy is a free and open-source software written by me (Joe Zeitouny) a software engineer and a sailor that empowers you to monitors systems on board your vessel with its suite of tools and log various metrics in a digital form. I believe that everyone should have access to these powerful tools and since most of boat owners have a multitude of similar systems installed on board already; Technology nowadays can help with monitoring all of these systems from from a central point.

I do offer however installation, configuration, and customization services for BoatBuddy to help you get up and running quickly and efficiently. I will work with you to ensure that this software is tailored to your specific needs, so you can get the most out of it.

In addition, I offer a tiered subscription model that provides exclusive access to support. As a subscriber, you'll get personalized assistance with any questions or issues you may have with BoatBuddy. I will work with you to find solutions that meet your unique needs and ensure that you're getting the most out of your subscription.

My goal is to provide you with the best possible experience using BoatBuddy. Whether you're just getting started or you're a seasoned user, I am here to help you every step of the way. Contact me today to learn more about my services and subscription options!
